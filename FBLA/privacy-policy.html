Privacy Policy

Superior accounting and consulting services customized for the needs of your business

Your Privacy is Important to Us

We highly value your trust and confidence in us and want to assure you that your personal information is kept completely confidential by us. As one of the nations top CPA firms we respect the information you entrust us with and go to great lengths to make sure that it is secure at all times.

This Privacy Policy summarizes what information we collect about you, the limited times when we may share it with others, and how we protect your privacy.

Types of Personal Information We Collect

In order to meet your needs in the course of tax preparation, tax and financial planning, and tax compliance engagements, we need to collect a multitude of personal information about you.

We can receive information in various ways. These include but are not limited to information shared in person, over the phone, mail, email and any information on tax preperations worksheet. Information we get may include, your name, address, birth date, social security number, income and investment sources and amounts, and other tax and financial attributes about you. Information about any of your transactions with us, our affiliates, or others. Such information may include, investment allocations and performance history, investment objectives, account balances, and payment history.

All the information we keep is kept secure for your access whenever you want through our online portal.We may retain the content of your email messages, your email address, and our subsequent response to you.

Parties to whom we disclose information

We may share some information with our accountants to assist in our contact with you and to aid in providing tax and financial planning solutions to you. We understand that some information can be very sensitive, thus we only share nesscary information with junior accountants and any information we feel may be too sensitive to share is handled only by the senior accountants at Accuracy. We do not share personal information with unaffiliated third parties without your express consent, except as permitted by law and as described below.

The law permits disclosure in certain circumstances, such as if we must share information to protect against fraud or to protect against credit risk, in response to a court subpoena, and sharing with others who may be assisting us in compliance with professional standards or who assist us in providing tax preparation and other services to you.

We may also share information to some third parties for marketing and research, but throughout this process your identity remains anonymous. Thus minimizing the chance of an information leak.

For your protection, our agreements with these entities require that they also keep your personal information in the strictest of confidence, just as we do.

Protecting the confidentiality and security of your information

Keeping your information confidential and secure is of utmost importance to us. In doing so we maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to guard your personal information. In doing so, we restrict all access to your personal information except to those employees who are crucial for your CPA needs. All our employees are bound by internal confidentiality policies and are subject to disciplinary action for any policy violations. Should your status become inactive or should our relationship end, we will continue to protect the confidentiality and security of your personal information in accordance with this privacy policy.

Our pledge to you

Here at Accuracy, we consider our customers more than just customers. We treat them like family and as family it is our ethical responsibility to demand the highest regard towards the confidentiality of your personal information. We promise to protect your personal information at all costs, use it appropriately, and accomplish our engagements with you in such manner as to always maintain your trust and confidence. Please call any of our local Accuracy offices or your client service contact if you have any questions.
